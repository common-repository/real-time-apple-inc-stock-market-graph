== Description ==

Display Apple's real time stock graph generated from Google Finance. 
Not only can you show Apple's stock graph in your sidebar you can enable advanced features that allow your visitors to enter any two company names and see their general popularity stats according to Google Trends. 
The widget generates side by side comparisons so you visitors and visualize which companies are being searched for more online. 
Install this widget and give your visitors a reason to talk great about their experience with your web site!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-apple.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory

2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

3. Go to Widgets (under Appearance) and drag "WP Real time Apple,Inc. Stock Market Graph" to the sidebar

4. Open "Real time Apple,Inc. Stock Market Graph" in sidebar and fill in the title, the first market's name and the second market's name.
5. Save and close sidebar widget.
